Together, Liquid AI, AMD, and Robotec.ai have deployed compact foundation models for autonomous agentic robotics: showcasing a specialized 3-billion parameter Liquid vision-language model (LFM2-VL-3B), running efficiently on AMD Ryzen™ AI processors, to enable real-time multimodal perception and decision-making at the edge.
